## **Introduction**

* **Flexibility:** Suno is intelligent; any style descriptor, even those not listed here, can potentially be interpreted. Feel free to invent your own or combine styles creatively.  
* **Specificity:** Bonus points for using styles that are obscure, highly specific, historically accurate, or even conceptually paradoxical (like 16-bit roots reggae).  
* **LLM Focus:** Styles that reference digital concepts, AI, or language processing might yield interesting results.  
* **Combination:** The real power comes from combining these tags (e.g., acoustic chicago blues algorave, ambient trance folk, punk polka).
