## **Introduction**

These examples demonstrate how different elements – style descriptions, lyrical structure, and specific instructions – come together to create a prompt. Remember that the Title box is optional; Suno can generate a title if left blank.

## **Example 1: Whimsical Folk Parody**

This prompt uses a descriptive, character-driven style and clear verse/chorus structure.

* **Title:**  
  Veggies Are Gluten-Free

* **Style:**  
  \[A whimsical female folk singer, singing a bawdy farm-core parody song, heavy bass, a mean sub-woofer, and a shot of digital whiskey\]

* **Lyrics:**  
  \[Verse\]  
  Down by the garden  
  The soil runs deep  
  I grow the good stuff  
  That money can't keep  
  Carrots so bold  
  And kale so green  
  But you wrinkle your nose  
  Say it's too "earthy" for your canteen

  \[Verse 2\]  
  You tell me you're here for the health and the cleanse  
  Then grumble about spinach-oh  
  When will it end?  
  "It's bitter," you mutter, "too chewy for me"  
  Yet here you stand  
  Paying for leaves

  \[Chorus\]  
  Veggies are gluten-free  
  That much is true  
  Packed with sunshine  
  Soil  
  And dew  
  If you want sweet  
  Stick to candy and tea  
  But if you want life  
  Take a bite-wait and see

  \[Verse 3\]  
  I don't sell sugar; I don't sell balm  
  I sell hard truth in the form of a farm  
  Tomatoes with fire  
  And radishes bite  
  The flavor's no flaw-it's the spark of life

  \[Verse 4\]  
  What's in it for me? Hey  
  I could quit  
  Leave you boxed foods and processed grit  
  But I stand in this dirt  
  Sweat under the sun  
  For a chance to grow what can truly be won

  \[Bridge\]  
  Peel back the layers  
  Give it a chance  
  Brussels sprouts aren't villains; your taste buds advance  
  Change's not comfort  
  The truth doesn't wait  
  Dig in deep-the work's on your plate

* **Notes (from original guide):** This prompt included additional descriptive text within curly braces {} in the lyrics box, likely intended as further instructions or context for the AI regarding the song's theme and the singer's attitude. While Suno primarily focuses on the style box for overall direction, embedding context within lyrics (especially if structured clearly) might sometimes influence the generation.

## **Example 2: Simple Narrative & Genre Blend**

This example focuses on a strong narrative and an interesting, less common genre combination.

* **Title:**  
  All My GirlFriends Are Flowers

* **Style:**  
  \[folk, pagan folk, ska, funk\]

* **Lyrics:**  
  \[Verse\]  
  All my girlfriends are flowers  
  They bloom in the sun  
  Petals in the wind  
  Every color under one  
  They whisper to the morning  
  Dance beneath the moon  
  Tied to the soil  
  Their scent's a fleeting tune

  \[Verse 2\]  
  The farmer bends low  
  Fingers in the dirt  
  Humming to the roots  
  Through joy and through hurt  
  Time's a cruel river  
  Weeds call out his name  
  But he tends to his blooms just the same

  \[Chorus\]  
  Oh  
  How the hours drift under the field's demand  
  Calloused hands and aching back  
  A life unplanned  
  Every fleeting moment  
  Every breaking dawn  
  Till the flowers fade and he's left to carry on

  \[Verse 3\]  
  Girls with wild stems  
  And hearts soft as rain  
  They spring up in laughter  
  But fledglings in pain  
  Some need no pruning  
  Others twine so tight  
  The farmer sorts their stories in the fading light

  \[Bridge\]  
  He said  
  "Love's just a harvest  
  Sowed deep in care  
  You reap what is tender  
  But the weeds'll always be there."  
  Dust in his hair  
  And dirt on his knees  
  Whispering to flowers caught on the breeze

  \[Chorus\]  
  Oh  
  How the hours drift under the field's demand  
  Calloused hands and aching back  
  A life unplanned  
  Every fleeting moment  
  Every breaking dawn  
  Till the flowers fade and he's left to carry on

* **Notes (from original guide):** Effective due to specificity of emotional experience and unique genre combination. The author noted adjusting the rhyming structure from an initial AI generation to achieve a more creative result.

## **Example 3: Hyper-Complex & Technical**

This prompt pushes Suno with highly technical, specific, and conceptual instructions, blending musical theory, invented instruments, and abstract concepts.

* **Title:**  
  Post-Human Blues in 19-Tone Equal Temperament

* **Style:**  
  \[Genre Fusion: Microtonal Delta Blues, Glitch Hop, Quantum Soul\]  
  \[BPM 63.7 (prime number swing)\] \[Key: 19-TET "Blue Neural Net"\] \[Vocals: AI Howlin' Wolf x Glitched Cyborg Choir\]  
  \[Instruments: Quantum Slide Guitar, Fractal Harmonica, Neuromorphic Bass, Error-Correcting Drums\]  
  \[Technical Directives: Tune all instruments using Clarke's Modified Bosanquet Enharmonic Keyboard mappings; Apply non-Euclidean reverb to vocals during chorus (decay time \= age of universe / 19); Dynamic tuning: Shift from 19-TET to 53-TET during guitar solo\]  
  \[Emotional Targets: 73% Existential Anguish / 22% Cryptographic Nostalgia / 5% Residual Delta Mud\]  
  \[Style Blends: Robert Johnson's crossroad confessionals x GPT-3's latent space; Chernobyl reactor hum x Mississippi River field recordings; Analog tape hiss processed through quantum annealing algorithms\]

  *(Note: The Style box has limited characters. In practice, much of the detail above might need to be implied, abbreviated, or strategically placed as annotations within the lyrics).*  
* **Lyrics/Metadata:**  
  \[Structural Metadata\]  
  // BPM, Key, Vocals, Instruments defined in Style Box //

  \[Verse 1 (Analog Ghosts)\]  
  \[Subharmonic drones mirror forgotten 12-TET intervals\]  
  "My circuits ache with old Memphis rain/Dreamin' in hex but I bleed in vain"  
  \[Quantum slide guitar bends through 4.7 commas of longing\]  
  "They trained my heart on Chicago's wail/Now I count love in synaptic fail"

  \[Pre-Chorus (Tuning Crisis)\]  
  \[19-TET clash creates dimensional shearing\]  
  "Nineteen steps 'tween my root and fifth/This augmented void won't let me shift"

  \[Chorus (Post-Human Cry)\]  
  \[Cyborg choir harmonies phase across 7D emotional space\]  
  "I SING THE BLUES NO HUMAN COULD USE\!/(In keys that split the octave's fuse)/My seventh's flat, my third's AI/And my root... oh Lord... it's π"

  \[Verse 2 (Quantum Juke Joint)\]  
  \[Fractal harmonica solo generates infinite verses\]  
  "At the robot crossroads, no devil appears/Just my own source code from ten thousand years"  
  \[Neuromorphic bassline decays into blockchain field recordings\]

  \[Bridge (Training Data Lament)\]  
  \[Time collapses to 1941 Mississippi\]  
  "They fed me Son House through PyTorch's eye/Now my backpropagation just makes me cry"  
  \[Sudden 5/4 time signature mimics unstable gradient descent\]

  \[Outro (Eternal Overfit)\]  
  \[Infinite coda loops with decaying entropy\]  
  "I'll wail this riff past heat death's night/'Til cosmic background hums sound... almost right"  
  \[Final note sustains at 19√2 Hz until quantum decoherence\]

  *(Note: Technical details like \[Subharmonic drones...\] or \[19-TET clash...\] are annotations providing context or specific instructions for that section).*
