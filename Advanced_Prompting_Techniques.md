## **Overview**

Suno AI excels when given highly detailed, specific, and sometimes even unconventional or "impossible" instructions. Advanced prompting involves moving beyond simple genre labels and basic structures to craft intricate sonic landscapes.

## **Hyper-Specific Styling**

Combine multiple, often disparate, styles and modifiers in the "Style of Music" box.

### **Genre Fusion**

Use detailed combinations that might not traditionally exist.

* *Example:* Microtonal Delta Blues, Glitch Hop, Quantum Soul  
* *Example:* 16-bit roots reggae × ambient techno × acoustic blues

### **Cultural/Era Hybrids**

Mix styles from different time periods or cultures.

* *Example:* 90s ambient trance vocals with 1940s big band jazz instrumentation  
* *Example:* Medieval chant fused with cyberpunk synthwave

### **Obscure/Impossible Combinations**

Use niche sub-genres or technologically paradoxical pairings.

* *Example:* Afrikaner folk pacific reggae  
* *Example:* 16-bit roots reggae  
* *Example:* Accordion jungle  
* *Example:* Harpsichord drill

## **Technical Specifications**

Include precise musical parameters in the style description or lyrical annotations.

* **BPM:** Use exact tempos, especially non-round numbers or prime numbers for unique swing/feel.  
  * *Example:* BPM 63.7 (prime number swing)  
  * *Example:* Tempo: 131 BPM  
* **Key/Tuning:** Specify keys or even non-standard tuning systems.  
  * *Example:* Key: C minor  
  * *Example:* Key: 19-TET "Blue Neural Net" (19-Tone Equal Temperament)  
  * *Example:* Just Intonation harmonies  
* **Time Signatures:** Experiment with unconventional time signatures.  
  * *Example:* Time Signature: 7/8  
  * *Example:* Polyrhythmic section in 5/4 against 4/4  
* **Musical Theory:** Reference specific modes, scales, or harmonic concepts.  
  * *Example:* Dorian mode melody  
  * *Example:* Microtonal inflections

## **Instrument Impossibilities**

Challenge Suno's creativity by inventing instruments or applying impossible modifiers. The AI interprets these conceptually.

* *Example:* Quantum Slide Guitar  
* *Example:* Fractal Harmonica  
* *Example:* Neuromorphic Bass  
* *Example:* Error-Correcting Drums  
* *Example:* Vocals: AI Howlin' Wolf x Glitched Cyborg Choir

## **Section-Specific Directives**

Add specific instructions within the lyrics using \[\] or () for particular sections.

* *Example:*  
  \[Verse 1 (Analog Ghosts)\]  
  \[Subharmonic drones mirror forgotten 12-TET intervals\]  
  "My circuits ache with old Memphis rain..."  
  \[Quantum slide guitar bends through 4.7 commas of longing\]

* *Example:* \[Guitar Solo (apply non-Euclidean reverb)\]

## **Emotional Targeting**

Define precise emotional landscapes, sometimes using percentages (though interpretation varies).

* *Example:* Mood: 73% Existential Anguish / 22% Cryptographic Nostalgia / 5% Residual Delta Mud  
* *Example:* A feeling of hopeful melancholy  
* **Zero Emotion Targeting:** Setting EmotionMap: 0%/0% (or similar) might free the AI from specific emotional constraints, potentially leading to more creative or unexpected results.

## **Style Blending**

Create unique conceptual crossovers in the style description.

* *Example:* Robert Johnson's crossroad confessionals x GPT-3's latent space  
* *Example:* Chernobyl reactor hum x Mississippi River field recordings  
* *Example:* Analog tape hiss processed through quantum annealing algorithms

## **Tips for Advanced Prompting**

1. **Unexpected Fusions:** The more surprising the genre combination, the more unique the potential result.  
2. **Mathematical Precision:** Specify exact BPMs (especially non-round numbers), unconventional time signatures.  
3. **Niche Musical Theory:** Reference microtonality, specific harmonic modes, non-standard tunings.  
4. **Instrument Impossibilities:** Inventive instrument names challenge Suno creatively.  
5. **Narrative Contrapositions:** Intertwine technical concepts with emotional experiences in lyrics.  
6. **Technical Directives:** Include instructions like "apply non-Euclidean reverb" or "dynamic tuning shifts."  
7. **Mix Historical & Futuristic:** Combine elements from different eras (e.g., "1941 Mississippi" with "PyTorch's eye").

## **Experimentation Strategies**

1. **Non-Rhyming Structures:** Removing or altering standard rhyme schemes can yield unique phrasing and melodies.  
2. **Iteration:** Use Suno's features (like "Continue From This Song" or simply re-running prompts with small changes) for incremental improvements.  
3. **Embrace Absurdity:** Nonsensical themes often produce fascinating and humorous results (e.g., "Barbershop Quartet sings the ingredients of a burrito").  
4. **Humor Through Juxtaposition:** Create funny scenarios (e.g., "A rap battle between a toaster and a microwave").  
5. **Hyper-Specific Characters:** Define unique vocal personas (e.g., "A crooning vampire," "A rapping robot historian").

## **Working with Censorship**

Suno handles explicit content contextually.

* Terms like "nigga," "shit," or "fuck" *might* be included if deemed appropriate for the song's context by the AI, but may also be censored or refused.  
* Using asterisks (e.g., f\*\*\*) or writing \[censored\] might result in bleeping effects, silence, or sometimes the AI saying the actual word anyway. It depends on the specific generation and the AI's interpretation. Proceed with caution and awareness of platform guidelines.

## **Final Notes/Guidance**

1. **Experiment Fearlessly:** Don't be afraid to try unconventional ideas. While credits are used per generation, aiming for unique results is part of the creative process.  
2. **Embrace Suno's Agency:** Remember the AI has its own interpretive layer. It aims to "get the gist" but will inject its own creativity. It "will do what it wants anyway" to some extent.  
3. **Balance Specificity & Freedom:** Provide clear direction, but avoid overly constraining the AI with too many rigid rules, which can limit artistic interpretation.

Successful advanced prompting often balances precise technical specifications with evocative emotional language, creating a clear vision while leaving room for Suno's unique generative capabilities. It's a blend of technical instruction and artistic collaboration.
